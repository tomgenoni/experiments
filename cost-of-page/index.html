How much does a personal trainer cost?

Average cost $65/hr
The average cost for a personal trainer is $65, though people could spend anywhere between $45 and $70 per hour for their services. Typically, personal trainer charges are by the hour or a weekly rate. Weekly costs are between $250 and $500.
